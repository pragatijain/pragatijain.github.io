Onboarding Documentation

Context

The Applied Computing Foundation (ACF) is an education-based non-profit organization that teaches technology and entrepreneurship to middle and high school students through its ACF Start-up Team program.

An ACF start-up team, composed of coaches and up to four students, participates in a four-season cycle to deliver a technological solution that promotes their business idea and a web page that hosts their business solution. Each season lasts for 14 weeks, during which students and coaches meet for two hours each week at a time established at the beginning of the season.

Students' ultimate goal is to apply and compete in competitions such as the US Congressional App Challenge and Conrad Challenge, as well as to create a personal portfolio they can use to share their growth stories during college applications.

Problem

To prepare for their classes, ACF start-up coaches participated at weekly team meetings, and scavenged through notes from different start-up teams to account for the various tasks required for achieving the entrepreneurship, technical, and outreach goals. The information was dispersed, and the process was lengthy.

It was essential to create a document that shares:

  • Learning objectives and task checklists per goal for each season.
  • Classroom readiness checklists for management, instructors, and students for each season.
  • Classroom tools and resources required to support classroom learnings.
  • Best practices for managing the ACF start-up classroom online sessions.

Contribution

Reflecting on my one-year journey as a part-time ACF start-up coach, I was brought on board to create an onboarding document to improve the ACF start-up coach onboarding experience for each season. I interviewed different ACF start-up coaches and pivoted the document to the user's needs. For instance, besides creating an onboarding document, I also created an ACF Goal tracker template that included specific tasks per goal along with a suggested timeline for each season. The template can be personalized by a team to support their team goals.

Over the course of two months, I:

  • Reviewed the existing content, blogs, and medium articles submitted by different ACF start-up teams and coaches to understand their learning experience.
  • Interviewed different stakeholders including the founders, advisors, and start-up coaches to understand the pain points and challenges.
  • Created checklists and process maps to support the onboarding process for start-up coaches and students.
  • Developed different collaterals and templates to support the ACF start-up classroom learnings to facilitate student project management, time management, and collaboration.
  • Initiated a style guide to capture key decisions around content styles.
  • Created a video script mockup to suggest content must-haves.

Impact

  • Developed an onboarding document to provide comprehensive training and support for the successful onboarding of an Applied Computing Foundation (ACF) start-up coach. The document received positive feedback from the founders and veteran ACF start-up coaches and is used to onboard new hires. This document will be updated every six months to reflect feedback from ACF start-up coaches.
  • Collaborating with the team to create a series of videos to improve the onboarding experience.
